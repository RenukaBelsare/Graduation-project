Blood bank


A blood bank is a center where blood gathered as a result of blood donation is stored & preserved for later use in blood transfusion.The term blood bank typically refer to division of a hospital where the storage of blood,product occurs & where proper testing is perform.however,its some times refers to a collection center & indeed some hospitals also perform collection.

Who can give blood

  • Most people can give blood you can give blood if you:

  • are fit & healthy.

  • Weight between 7stone 12lbs & 25stone or 50kg & 160kg.

  • are aged between 17 & 66(or 70 if you have blood give n before).

  • are over 70& have given blood in last 2 years.
